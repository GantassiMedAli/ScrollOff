1. Introduction

At ScrollOff, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our digital wellness platform and services.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our services.

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • Usage data and interaction patterns with our platform
  • Device information and technical identifiers
  • Location data (with your consent)
  • Content you create, upload, or share on our platform

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes to provide, maintain, and improve our services.

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services and user experience
  • To personalize your experience and deliver relevant content
  • To process transactions and send related information
  • To send you updates, newsletters, and promotional communications
  • To respond to your inquiries, comments, and support requests
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We use a combination of technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to ensure the security of your data.

Our Security Measures Include:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data transmission
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure data storage with access controls and monitoring
  • Compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular staff training on data protection best practices
